Internship

Time: Freshman Summer Vacation

National Central University

Time: Sophomore Summer Vacation
Department: Soil Hydrology Laboratory
Project title: Groundwater level changes induced by 1999 Chi-Chi earthquake in Choshui River Alluvial fan
Abstract: This study aims to quantifies and analyzes the variations in groundwater levels prior to and following the 1999 Chi-Chi earthquake, understand the spatial distribution of groundwater level changes and their underlying causative factors. The result indicates that local geological conditions, topography, and water potential have greater influence than fault distance on groundwater levels. Fractures converge groundwater levels, and flow direction depends on aquifer potential energy.
